Welcome to Elemental Alchemies, a digital portfolio of Ayurvedic gourmet creations that merge ancient wisdom with modern indulgence.  
This repository celebrates the art of Functional Decadence — desserts and elixirs that heal, balance, and delight.

---

✨ Philosophy
Ayurveda teaches that food is medicine, and indulgence can be therapeutic when aligned with elemental balance.  
Each recipe here is designed to harmonize the five elements — Earth, Water, Fire, Air, and Ether — through taste, texture, and intention.

I believe luxury can be functional, and wellness can be sensual.  
Every creation is a dialogue between science and spirit, nourishment and pleasure.
